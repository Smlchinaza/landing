WHO I AM

Marius Nilsen is a highly experienced attorney with more than 18 years of practice in California. He founded Nilsen Law Firm to deliver personalized, strategic legal representation that combines courtroom excellence with practical business insight.

After earning his Juris Doctor from UCLA School of Law, Marius served as a judicial clerk and later practiced at a major Los Angeles firm before opening his own practice. His client-first philosophy, meticulous preparation, and proven track record have earned him the respect of peers and the gratitude of hundreds of satisfied clients.

Whether negotiating complex business deals, guiding families through sensitive transitions, or advocating in court, Mr. Nilsen brings calm confidence and unwavering commitment to every matter.
